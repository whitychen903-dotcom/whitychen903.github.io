Official HIGE DANdism

J-Pop / Piano Rock

A Japanese piano rock band formed in 2012 in Shimane Prefecture by four childhood friends. The band name means 'even when we're old enough for beards to suit us, we want to keep making music that excites everyone.' With Satoshi Fujihara's distinctive vocals and piano at its core, they blend rock, R&B, and pop. Hit songs include 'Pretender,' 'I LOVE...,' and 'Subtitle.'
