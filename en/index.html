Mrs. GREEN APPLE

#1

A three-member rock band formed in 2013, consisting of Motoki Omori (Vo/Gt), Hiloto Wakai (Gt), and Ryoka Fujisawa (Key). 'Mrs.' represents delicate feminine perspective, while 'GREEN APPLE' symbolizes unripe youth. Hit songs include 'Ao to Natsu,' 'Tenbyou no Uta,' 'Dance Hall,' and 'Lilac,' having won the Japan Record Award three consecutive years.
